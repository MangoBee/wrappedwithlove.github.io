About the florist

Crafting bespoke arrangements is my specialty, and I’m dedicated to creating each design with quality products and meticulous care. I'm a studio floral designer based in Riverside, California; designing floral arrangements for everyday occasions, weddings, memorials, and special events. We offer flower subscriptions to receive flowers at your convenience. At 21 years old, I'm the owner of Florist Wrapped With Love, eager to inspire and help others share special moments and convey feelings through art. My mission in starting my company is to provide fresh flowers and artfully handcrafted arrangements to all, promoting self-care, building community, and fostering closeness with loved ones. At Florist Wrapped With Love, we deliver quality flower arrangements and excellent service.
